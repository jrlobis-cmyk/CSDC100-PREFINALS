Ethical Use of AI

1. Responsible Use in the Academe

AI should be used as a learning support tool ‚Äî not as a replacement for your actual work.

2. Avoiding Plagiarism

Always rewrite AI-generated text using your own understanding and cite the tool used.

3. Accuracy and Verification

Always double-check AI outputs because they can sometimes produce incorrect answers.

4. Bias and Fairness

AI models are trained on human data, meaning biases may appear. Review critically.

5. Personal Guidelines

  • Use AI to study, not cheat.
  • Always verify answers with real sources.
  • Do not submit AI content without editing.
  • Use AI to improve, not replace, your skills.
