Connection to AI Developers

The same goes for developers who use AI. Even if they use AI tools for coding or generating ideas, the final product reflects their own, because they are the one who promt it:

  • Logic and problem-solving
  • Creativity
  • Debugging skills
  • Understanding of systems
  • Originality of design
  • Integrity and work ethic

AI may assist them like how a camera assists a photographer but it does not replace human talent. Skilled developers still need to understand, refine, debug, and think critically.
